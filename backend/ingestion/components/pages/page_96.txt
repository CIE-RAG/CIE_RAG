Tips/Tricks – Some useful Practical Tips in Validating You Hypothesis
• Find ‘real’ customer(s)
⚬‘Real’: who you (potentially) plan to sell your solution
• While your hypothesis may target global/country/city, test it ‘near’ you
⚬”think global, test local” first (you can always expand this out later)
• How to test your hypothesis?
⚬Verbal is good but ”pretotype” is better! (people pay attention if you show something)
⚬Pretotype is none of the following: expensive, takes time to build, complex!
• Develop customer interviewing skills (see CD Toolkit)
⚬See ”Tips and Tricks” , videos and other resources listed in subsequent slides
